Privacy Notice

Last updated: June 2026

JSA Capital S.L., operating under the MetaXAgora brand (part of Metaxium Group, "we"), is responsible for the processing of your personal data under the Mexican Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (LFPDPPP). This notice explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over it.

In short: we use your information only to evaluate your capacity request and, if there's a fit, follow up with you directly. We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for purposes other than those described here.

1. Personal data we collect

When you complete our capacity inquiry form, we collect:

  • Contact and identification data: name, email, company
  • Information about your organization and needs: type of organization, capacity required, timeline, and any additional notes you provide

We do not collect sensitive personal data, nor do we request it at any point.

2. Purposes of processing

We use your data for the following purposes, necessary for the relationship you establish with us when submitting the form:

  • Assessing whether your capacity needs match our infrastructure
  • Contacting you to discuss a potential colocation agreement
  • Routing your inquiry to MetaXTerra if it relates to land instead of compute capacity

We do not use your data to send third-party advertising, nor do we share it for commercial purposes unrelated to the Metaxium Group.

3. Data transfer

Your data may be shared within the Metaxium Group (MetaXTerra, MetaXAgora, MetaXVolt and Metaxium) when relevant to your inquiry. Outside of this case, we do not transfer your data to third parties without your consent, unless required by a competent authority under law.

4. How your information is stored

Information submitted through our form is received directly by our team and kept in our internal email and contact management systems, with restricted access.

5. Your rights (ARCO)

You have the right to Access, Rectify, Cancel, or Object to the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw your consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us directly — see details below.

6. Cookies and similar technologies

This site does not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics tools.

7. Changes to this notice

We may update this Privacy Notice periodically, reflecting any changes on this same page.
