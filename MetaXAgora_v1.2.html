What we provide

The facility. Nothing more, nothing less.

We are infrastructure landlords. We provide the physical layer so your team can focus on what runs inside it. No IT services, no managed cloud — pure facility.

Guaranteed power

Reserved capacity in kW. You pay for what you commit, not what you consume. Diesel backup N+1 with automatic transfer in under 10 seconds.

Carrier-neutral connectivity

Meet-me room with a minimum of two independent fiber carriers. You choose your ISP. We provide the passive infrastructure and the interconnection point.

Precision cooling

CRAH units maintaining 18–27°C and 40–60% RH per ASHRAE A1. Designed for the density you need — standard racks to high-density rows.

Physical security

Biometric and RFID access control, CCTV on every aisle, mantrap entry, perimeter fencing and 24/7 on-site security personnel.

24/7 facility monitoring

Round-the-clock monitoring of power, temperature, humidity and access. NOC alerts and remote hands available on request.

Remote hands

On-site technical support for physical tasks — cable management, hardware swap, visual inspection. Billed by the hour, available 24/7.

The model

Clear boundaries. Simple contract.

We define exactly what we deliver and what you bring. No ambiguity, no scope creep. The contract reflects this boundary precisely.
